Long before words were written, the letters lived in two worlds
In the valley below, lived the lowercase letters

The lowercase were cheerful, happy, curious, and always worked together. They made stories, wrote letters between friends, and filled countless books with creations.
But... There's the UPPERCASE letters, who lived up high above them. The capitals were proud letters. They stood tall and always turned attention towards them. They had kings, created wars, and won battles.
Every capital above seeked to be the strongest there could be.

For centuries, neither cases crossed paths with each other
The lowercase letters thought the capitals were too bloodlusted.
While the capitals thought the lowercase letters were too relaxed

But the future of language couldn't exist without both.
The letters had to eventually agree to become one big page.
