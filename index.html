Introducing.... Facey font!

Facey font is unique since, well it gives every letter a face lol.
But this font is more than that... this font has a story!
If you experiment around with the letters, you may notice something interesting!
For example:

hello! i'm feeling super happy!


Taking a closer look at the text, you can see that each letters is a face!
but there's a but more to this font than just that
For example:

HEY! I'M VERY VERY ANGRY!!!


You can see that all the capital letters are angry 😡
There isn't a specific reason as to why I made lowercase letters happy, and uppercase angry.
Though I thought it would be interesting to see a font where both cases were different in some way.

Also here's how it looks like with using both cases together

The Quick Brown Fox Jumped Over The Lazy Dog


When you first look at the font, it may seem like each letter just has a bunch of artifacts in it.
Though I think that's what makes it somewhat unique, as it makes the font stand out from other regular fonts.
