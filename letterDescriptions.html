Here is every character in the font, and a description for every single one!

a - This letter could be used for a replacement for the famous :) face!

b - This one is a simpler version of :b, a very chill character!

c - If you've seen those big yellow smiling emoji on like kids tv shows. Then this would basically be that!

d - Similar to the previous one, this is a simpler version of d:

e - This could be used as a mocking face or when being devious.

f - This letter was inspired by that one facebook logo art I was saw a long back while ago. Could be used to show something related to doomscrolling or back pain lol

g - Now this is a cool font... get it? cause it has sunglasses on?.. Moving on, this is on the same level as the sunglasses emoji!

h - Now this is a funky letter. I would imagine someone using this when they see something they want to eat (like a pancake with ice cream)

i - I think this letter looks perfectly as it's used. It's an individual standing. Just like how people use the letter to refer themselves.

j - This is just another variation of i, except it's got a bit more curve it.

k - this is probably the most unique lowercase letter in the font. This letter could be used to present something great!

l - I see this letter as just "meh" or just a neutral expression.

m - This is just a :3 face but turned 45 degrees anti clockwise lol

n - This is also just c, but this time turned 45 degrees clockwise!

o - I wouls use this leter to show my shocked reaction at something. Like "Whaaat?! o"

p - the iconic :p face. Another simple letter to use.

q - same as p, but flipped horizontally

r - this one is interesting. It's a smiley face but... diagonal?!

s - now this is truly revolutionary! 2 smiley faces in one!!

t - kind of a mix of the T-pose, but also a person sitting down.

u - the letter c but 45 degrees anti-clockwise. But this one is the closest to a proper sided smiley face!

v - It's like :>, but also on the correct side like "u"

w - It's like v, but twice the v's. Also the letter kinda reminds of me of an old person.

x - now THIS is the peak of all the letters, quadruple faces!!!

y - like g, but instead of cool it's just happy u

z - Probably the closest letter to the capital letter variants. Could argue that "e" is closer, but this I would say is closer. This one also looks like it's blushing lol




I don't have much to say about the capital letters, as they are quite similar in use.
But I'll highlight the ones that stand out the most! (at least in my option. Different capital letters may stand out for other users!)

E - Probably the most evil character in this entire font. Covered in teeth, dangerous if approached V

F - Similar to E, but slightly safer to approach, also kind of looks like a half of E

H - The double faces return, but instead in parallel with each other, both angry

K - honestly this is probably the least angry/neutral capital letter among the capitals. nearly identical to the lowercase variant.

L - one of the few diagonal capitals, (other than Z). I also wanted to note that this one looks like it has 2 L's, which is pretty cool!

O - the inverse of the lowercase variant "o", the face is now inside the letter and is like an angry shouting face

Q - The most angry captial of them all. Clearly shows the anger in the face, and also resembles the a, but bigger and angrier.

T - I wanted to quickly also mention this. As it's like the lowercase variant but now just T-posing and angry.

Z - The last capital of the font. Like the lowercase variant, it does look like it's blushing. But it also doesn't really look angry (at least for me)
It does look like something that would be dangerous to approach. But ig another way to describe it would be kawaii lol.


Now last but not least, the special characters!

. - the smiley face, but in a small form factor!

; - one smiley face in a semi-colon

/ - basically the same as l, but diagonal!

? - The dot on this one has a shocked expression, similar to "o". Fits well with this character!

# - One of my favourite characters in this font. A game of tic tac toe inside a hastag!

$ - A face that's got money in it's eyes. This represents the money symbol well.

* - I've sometimes heard this referred to as a star. So I turned it into one! Also one of the few non-face characters in this font.

& - Since people use this as the word "and", I decided to quite literally turn it into the word!

" - The quotation marks are the eyes, with a little smile underneath u

1 - similar to the lowercase l, but kinda grinning.

2 - not sure what kinda smile this is, but it's an interesting one!

3 - litearlly the :3 cat face but now permanently turned into the letter 3!

4 - another one of the few diagonal faces, but looks kinda funny.

5 - Similar to 2, but this one looks like it has a hand coming out of it!

6 - an upside down g with eyes on the side, or number 6 with eyes. Your pick!

7 - It seems I have some returning joke of diagonal l's lol. Another one like that, but now it's a diagonal capital L

8 - This one reminds of spiderman's mask, with the big eyes and that smile.

9 - A combination of the number 9 and v put together!

My favourite character in this font would be #. Mainly cause it's got that tic tac toe game in it. But probably the most unique character in the font!

Another quick honorable mention is &, cause I thought it was funny the letter looks like the more literal use for it!
